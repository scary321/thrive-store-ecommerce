Our story

Less compromise. More planet.

Thrive is a curated marketplace for people who want to shop well without doing the homework themselves.

Thrive began with a simple frustration: shopping sustainably was too hard. Greenwashing was everywhere, and doing real research took hours per purchase.

So we built a marketplace that does the vetting for you. Every product is screened across three dimensions — materials, supply-chain ethics, and end-of-life impact — before it earns a place on the shelf. If it doesn't clear all three, it doesn't list. No exceptions, no "eco-ish."

Today we partner with 120+ independent makers across five categories, ship carbon-neutral on every order, and reinvest 1% of revenue into reforestation. We're small, profitable, and growing on our own terms.

Vetted, not vibes
